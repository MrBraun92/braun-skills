# Research Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure thorough research before writing.

## Topic Understanding

**Core question**: What is the main question or problem this article addresses?

**Audience knowledge level**: What does the target reader already know about this topic?

**Search intent**: Is the reader looking for information, comparison, tutorial, or inspiration?

## Source Gathering

**Primary sources** (3-5 minimum):
- Academic papers or industry reports
- Official documentation or whitepapers
- Expert interviews or quotes
- Original data or case studies

**Secondary sources** (2-3 for context):
- Reputable news outlets
- Industry blogs or thought leaders
- Competitor articles (for gap analysis)

## Fact Verification

For each key claim or statistic:
- Trace back to original source (not secondary reporting)
- Check publication date (prefer sources from last 2 years)
- Verify author credentials or publication authority
- Cross-reference with at least one other source

## Competitive Analysis

Review 3-5 top-ranking articles on the topic:
- What angles do they cover?
- What gaps or weaknesses exist?
- What unique perspective can we offer?
- What format works best (listicle, guide, case study)?

## Expert Input

When possible, include:
- Direct quotes from subject matter experts
- Original insights not found in existing content
- Real-world examples or case studies
- Contrarian or nuanced perspectives

## Data Collection

Gather supporting evidence:
- Statistics with clear sources and dates
- Examples or case studies (3-5 concrete instances)
- Screenshots or visuals to illustrate points
- Relevant tools or resources to recommend
