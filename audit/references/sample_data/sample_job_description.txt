# Sample Job Description

**Role:** Senior Backend Engineer
**Company:** Innovate Inc.
**Location:** Remote

Innovate Inc. is looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our growing infrastructure team. You will be responsible for designing, building, and maintaining the scalable, reliable services that power our core product.

**Responsibilities:**
- Design and develop microservices in Go.
- Manage and scale our Kubernetes-based infrastructure.
- Work with PostgreSQL databases to ensure data integrity and performance.
- Collaborate with frontend and product teams to ship new features.
- Mentor junior engineers.

**MUST-HAVE Qualifications:**
- 5+ years of professional software engineering experience.
- Strong proficiency in Go (Golang).
- Experience with containerization and orchestration (Docker, Kubernetes).
- Deep understanding of relational databases (e.g., PostgreSQL).

**NICE-TO-HAVE Qualifications:**
- Experience with gRPC for inter-service communication.
- Familiarity with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP).
- Experience with CI/CD pipelines (e.g., Jenkins, GitLab CI).
